My Intern Project
This summer, I interned at FPT Software Company as an Artificial Intelligence (AI) Developer. I was introduced with Computer Vision, a completely new field for me in Machine Learning world. I worked with data analysis several times in my Mathematical Modelling course, however, I have never had experiences in image processing or especially editing by code. In two months, I worked with User Interface/User Experience Design team to train a model predicting the content of UX flowchart cards and then generating a site-map based on object’s location. This sounds really interesting since my project can save a lot of time for Business Analytics (BA) team from delivery ideas from customer to UX/UI team. Consequently, BA can easier to sign a contract with customer since they can cut-down completing time of UX/UI team’s task from 2 weeks into 10 seconds. Hopefully with my model, BA team will only need to take a picture and then a sitemap will automatically appear.

At first, I was overwhelmed with all the technical knowledges that I need to learn. There are too many methods to recognize object and the process to optimize method is even more harder. After several meeting, we split our problem into small tasks.
